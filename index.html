Welcome to CU Apex 👋

A quick orientation before you run your first quarter — no login required

1 What is CU Apex?

CU Apex puts you in the executive chair of a real credit union — Lakeside Community Credit Union, seeded from actual NCUA 5300 call report data with $750M in assets.

Your team of four makes real management decisions each quarter and competes against peer institutions on a regulator-calibrated scorecard.

🏦 Your Institution This SessionLakeside Community CU · $743M Assets · 48,240 Members · Net Worth: 10.6% (well-capitalized)
Scenario: "The Great Unwind" — Rising rate environment, Q1 2023

2 Your Team's Four Roles

Each role has unique decisions and private information. No single person sees everything — you have to coordinate.

CEO — Strategy

Sets capital targets, growth posture, and staffing. Only role that sees all four executive summaries and submits the final round command.

CFO — Investments & Funding

Controls investment portfolio and deposit pricing. Sees full ALM risk picture others don't receive automatically.

CLO — Lending

Sets loan volumes, rates, and credit standards. Only role with full delinquency detail by product and vintage.

CMO — Members

Grows membership and protects the deposit base. Receives the private Member Satisfaction Index each quarter.

3 How a Round Works

Each round = one simulated quarter (3 months). In a real session, rounds run over days or weeks. In tutorial mode, all four roles are unlocked so you can explore everything at once.

  • Read the News Wire — Economic conditions scroll at the top. Fed rate, competition, HPI — they all affect your decisions.
  • Explore each role tab — CEO, CFO, CLO, CMO. Adjust sliders and toggles. Read the private intel boxes.
  • Hit ⚡ Process Quarter on the CEO tab — runs the calibrated engine instantly and populates all results.
  • Review Statements, CAMEL, Leaderboard — all update with your results.
📌 Tutorial ModeIn a real multiplayer session, each player only sees their own role's tab and must coordinate with teammates. Here you can see everything to learn how it all fits together.

4 How You're Scored — CAMEL + Growth

Your Apex Score (0–100) blends the CAMEL regulatory framework (75%) with a Growth Score (25%).

  • C — Capital (25%): Net worth ratio — your most critical metric.
  • A — Asset Quality (20%): Delinquency rate and underwriting discipline.
  • M — Management (20%): Efficiency ratio — how well you control expenses.
  • E — Earnings (20%): ROA and NIM vs. peer benchmarks.
  • L — Liquidity (15%): Can you fund obligations? Loan-to-share and liquidity ratio.
  • Growth Score (25%): Member growth, asset growth, and loan portfolio growth — balanced against safety.
⚠ The Most Important RuleIf Net Worth Ratio falls below 7.0%, your institution enters Prompt Corrective Action — growth authority is locked and a Capital Restoration Plan is required. Survival before growth.

5 Ready to Explore

The full calibrated simulation is live. Here's what to do first:

  • Browse the CEO tab — read the economic briefing. Set capital posture and growth mode.
  • Visit CFO — note that investment yields reflect the existing book (not new-money rates). CD pricing is the competitive battleground right now.
  • Visit CLO — read the private delinquency intelligence. Set FICO floors and provision expense carefully.
  • Visit CMO — read the Member Satisfaction Index. Marketing budget and channel mix affect both member count and deposit stability.
  • Hit ⚡ Process Quarter on the CEO tab to see full results.
